Coya Cottage - up to 5 guests

This traditional Japanese cottage is available as a private rental for 2 nights or more. Coya Cottage is tucked away in a peaceful green valley yet only 4 minutes walk from the popular Kisami Ohama Beach. Coya Cottage underwent a full renovation in 2015. We worked hard to retain the Japanese traditional designs and showa retro styling while updating the cottage with modern day conveniences.

Oku Cottage - up to 4 guests

This vacation house rental is on the grounds of Retreat wabi-sabi, walking distance to the popular Kisami Ohama beach. Oku Cottage sleeps 4 people in 2 tatami rooms, one upstairs, one on the second floor.

Wabisabi Guesthouse - up to 12 guests

The guesthouse at Retreat wabi-sabi is available as a private rental cottage. If you are traveling in a large group or would prefer the privacy you are welcome to book out the entire building: 5 guestrooms, large kitchen, indoor bathroom, outdoor shower. If you would like to host an event and/or make use of the cafe as well, please contact us direct. If the premises are rented privately, children are also welcome to stay at the guesthouse. (we do not rent individual rooms to families with small children, sorry.)
